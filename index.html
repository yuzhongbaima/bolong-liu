Dr. Bolong Liu is a researcher at the College of Intelligence Science and Technology, National University of Defense Technology, and also conducts cooperative postdoctoral research at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Beijing Institute of Technology under Prof. Changle Xiang, Member of the Chinese Academy of Engineering.

His research focuses on cross-domain mobile platforms, hydrodynamic optimization, CFD-based design, energy-saving mechanisms, data-driven configuration optimization, and intelligent control. His recent work addresses platforms operating across water, land, and air, with a systematic foundation in water-land amphibious platforms and an emerging direction in water-air cross-domain systems.

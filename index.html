Research Profile

Fluid engineering for platforms operating across water, land, and air

Dr. Liu focuses on digital design, hydrodynamic optimization, energy-saving mechanisms, and intelligent control of cross-domain platforms. His research has built a systematic foundation in water-land amphibious platforms and is expanding toward water-air cross-domain platform systems.

His work integrates hydrodynamics, CFD-based analysis, model-test validation, data-driven configuration optimization, fluid-structure interaction, and intelligent control to improve performance, reduce drag, and support the efficient design of complex mobile systems.
